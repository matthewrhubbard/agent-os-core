## Use Rules

- Lead with the findings, not the source-by-source scan.
- Include moderate evidence, not exhaustive evidence.
- Suggest files, edit shape, and draft wording for each real proposed change.
- Add only open, actionable items to `Changes-To-Make.md`.
- Put approved implementation work into `90-inbox/Approved-Changes.md`.
- Do not leave accepted items sitting in the live queue after implementation.
