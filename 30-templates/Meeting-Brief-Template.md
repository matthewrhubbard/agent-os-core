# Meeting Brief Template

Use this template to prepare for meetings with objectives, participants, context, and desired outcomes.

Related skill:
- `../20-skills/Execution/Meeting-Prep.md`

## When To Use

Use this template when:

- A meeting matters enough to prepare for intentionally
- You need a decision, alignment, input, or relationship progress
- Participant dynamics or context will materially affect the outcome

## Quality Check

Before using the brief, confirm:

- The meeting has a clear objective
- Participant dynamics are considered
- Only relevant context is included
- The desired next steps are visible
