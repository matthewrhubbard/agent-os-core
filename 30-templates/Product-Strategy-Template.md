# Product Strategy Template

Use this template to diagnose where product strategy effort should go and to articulate a clear recommendation.

Related skill:
- `../20-skills/Strategy/Product-Strategy.md`

## When To Use

Use this template when:

- a product team needs to decide where strategic effort belongs
- the question is whether to focus on features, growth, PMF expansion, or scalability
- leadership needs a concise explanation of the product strategy choice

## Quality Check

Before using the template output, confirm:

- the current product stage is named clearly
- the main bottleneck is explicit
- the chosen lens is justified
- alternatives and tradeoffs are visible
- the recommendation is actionable
