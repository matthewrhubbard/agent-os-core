# Decision Memo Template

Use this template to compare options, record tradeoffs, and recommend a decision.

Related skill:
- `../20-skills/Strategy/Decision-Memos.md`

## When To Use

Use this template when:

- A decision is high-stakes or hard to reverse
- Several plausible options exist
- You need to document reasoning, risk, and next steps

## Quality Check

Before using the memo, confirm:

- The real decision is named clearly
- The alternatives are serious, not strawmen
- Risks are explicit
- The recommendation is clear
