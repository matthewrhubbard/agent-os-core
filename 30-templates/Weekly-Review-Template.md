# Weekly Review Template

Use this template to review progress, open loops, lessons, and next priorities.

Related skills:
- `../20-skills/Execution/Weekly-Planning.md`
- `../20-skills/Execution/Retrospectives.md`

## When To Use

Use this template at the end of a week to capture what happened, what was learned, and what should change next week.

## Template

```md
# Weekly Review

## This Week's Intended Wins

- 

## What Actually Happened

- 

## What Worked

- 

## What Did Not Work

- 

## Open Loops

- 

## Lessons

- 

## Next Week's Priorities

- 

## Changes To Make

- 
```

## Quality Check

Before closing the review, confirm:

- Outcomes are described honestly
- Open loops are captured
- Lessons lead to concrete changes
