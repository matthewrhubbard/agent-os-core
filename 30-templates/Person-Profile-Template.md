# Person Profile Template

Use this template to record how an individual prefers to communicate, decide, and collaborate.

This template should work whether you keep collaborator notes in a public shared workspace or in a private people layer.

## When To Use

Use this template for people you collaborate with regularly or whose working style meaningfully affects execution.

## Quality Check

Before using the profile, confirm:

- Notes are based on observed working patterns
- The tone is respectful and useful
- The profile improves collaboration rather than labeling the person lazily
